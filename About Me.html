Who Am I

My name is Timothy James S Oriola, I am the creator of this website portfolio, I created this so that I have a convenient place to stash my works, personal and profesional, to be easily viewed by me and others. With the times changing and the world becoming more digital it made sense for me to transition to digital for the ease of storing and managing my works and information, especially with how fast life is today.

Currently I am looking for a career in writing, web design and photography as I have an interest in this fields, I am also aiming to improve my skills in other fields preferably drawing.
